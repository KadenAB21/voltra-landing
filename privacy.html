Privacy Policy

Voltra · Effective date: July 2, 2026 · Contact: hello@voltrasprint.com

This policy explains what information the Voltra app collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. We built Voltra to run on your device — the short version is that your training data stays there.

1. Information You Provide

  • Athlete profile: your name, biological sex (used for training calibration), events, personal bests and goals, training history, available equipment, training days, and meet dates.
  • Daily training data: readiness check-ins (sleep, soreness, stress, motivation, mood, confidence, leg freshness), logged session results, official meet results, and post-session feedback.
  • Optional profile photo: if you add one, it is stored on your device only.

2. Where Your Data Lives

At this time, all of your profile and training data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate a server that receives, stores, or processes your training data, and we cannot see it. Deleting the app deletes this data — it is not backed up on our systems, and it does not sync across devices. If we add cross-device sync in the future, we will update this policy first and describe exactly what is transmitted and stored.

3. Payments

Subscriptions are purchased through the Apple App Store or Google Play, and processed with RevenueCat, our subscription-management provider. We do not receive or store your payment card details. RevenueCat receives a device-scoped identifier and purchase status so the app knows your subscription is active. See RevenueCat's privacy policy, Apple's, and Google's.

4. What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your personal information — to anyone, ever.
  • We do not show third-party ads or share your data with advertisers.
  • We do not use your training data to train AI models.

5. Children and Teen Athletes

Voltra is designed for athletes 13 and older and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13; if you believe a child under 13 has used the Service, contact us at hello@voltrasprint.com and we will help remove the data. Athletes under 18 should use Voltra with the consent of a parent or legal guardian. Because training data is stored on the athlete's device, parents can review or delete it there at any time.

6. Your Choices and Rights

  • Access and correction: everything you have entered is visible and editable inside the app (Profile tab).
  • Deletion: deleting the app removes all locally stored data. Subscription records held by Apple/Google/RevenueCat follow their retention policies.
  • Depending on where you live (e.g., California, the EU/UK), you may have additional legal rights regarding personal information. Because we do not hold your training data on our servers, most requests can be fulfilled directly on your device; for anything else, email us.

7. Security

Your data is protected by your device's own security (passcode, biometrics, encryption at rest where enabled). Keep your device secured and up to date.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we change what data is collected or where it is stored — for example, when cross-device sync launches — we will update this page with a new effective date and notify you in the app before the change takes effect.
